Manufacturing

French manufacturing, the second-largest in Europe, accounts for 86% of total production, driven by food/beverages 13%, machinery 13%, metals 12%, and transport equipment 10%. Key industries include aerospace, automotive, chemicals, and luxury goods, emphasizing high-quality craftsmanship, modernization, and, increasingly, sustainable, local production

Tourism

The tourism sector accounts for roughly 8-9% of France's GDP, contributing over €250 billion to the national economy

PIB

France's nominal GDP is estimated to be approximately $3.16 trillion to $3.21 trillion USD in 2024–2025. It is consistently ranked as the world's 7th largest economy
