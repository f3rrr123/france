FRANCE

France is a country located in Western Europe, renowned for its rich history, cultural diversity, and significant influence on art, science, and gastronomy. With its capital in Paris, France is distinguished by its iconic monuments, varied landscapes, and key role in global politics and economics. Throughout history, it has been a cultural benchmark and an important destination for both tourism and European development.
